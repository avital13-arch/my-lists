# Ports and Locations Summary (Great Masurian Lakes PDF)

Notes:
- Price values are best-effort from publicly indexed web pages/snippets and may be seasonal or outdated.
- Many lakes/canals/towns do not have a single official price; those are marked accordingly.

## Route and Port Summary from the PDF
- Giżycko -> Mikołajki: 37 km route through canals, beginner-friendly.
- Mikołajki -> Ruciane-Nida: 19 km scenic route via Puszcza Piska and Lakes Mikołajskie/Bełdany.
- Giżycko -> Węgorzewo: 25 km northern route via Lake Mamry and Węgorzewski Canal.
- Mikołajki -> Pisz: 25 km route crossing Lake Śniardwy.
- Main port clusters: Giżycko, Węgorzewo, Mikołajki, Ryn, Ruciane-Nida, and Pisz areas.
